The Daily Jang
NATIONAL | WAR UPDATES | INTERNATIONAL | EDITORIAL

DEFENSE FORCES NEUTRALIZE THREE ENEMY BOMBERS

Retaliatory strike proves successful; Enemy aircraft wreckage found near border.

PILOT CAPTURED

In a daring maneuver earlier today, local forces successfully apprehended an enemy pilot who ejected after his plane was hit. The capture took place near the sector boundary where heavy shelling had been reported.

Authorities have confirmed that the prisoner is being treated according to international conventions. The morale of the local population remains high as news of the capture spread through the villages.

THE NATION STANDS UNITED

Hospital Visit Boosts Morale

High-ranking officials visited the Combined Military Hospital today to inquire about the health of soldiers injured in the recent skirmishes. The atmosphere was one of determination and courage, as the injured jawans expressed their eagerness to return to the front lines.

Doctors report that all patients are in stable condition and recovering well. The visit served as a significant morale booster, not just for the injured, but for the medical staff working round the clock to support the defense efforts.

WAR BULLETINS

  • Sialkot Sector: Heavy artillery exchange reported early morning.
  • Lahore Front: Situation remains calm but tense; citizens advised to observe blackout.
  • Air Support: PAF continues to patrol the skies, ensuring airspace safety.
  • Civil Defense: Emergency training sessions scheduled for civilians at 5:00 PM.

Editorial Notice

The editorial board urges all citizens to remain calm and avoid spreading rumors. Official broadcasts should be the only source of verified information during these critical times.
